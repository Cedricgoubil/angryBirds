If players cross over adjacent squares (horizontally or vertically), a battle begins. During combat, the game works is as follows:
Each player attacks in turn / The damage depends on the player's weapon / The player can choose to attack or defend against the next shot / If the player chooses to defend themselves, they sustain 50% less damage than normal / As soon as the life points of a player falls to 0, they lose.
